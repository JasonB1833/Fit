my background with fitness

I struggled with my weight for years growing up. During my early adulthood, I dedicated a lot of my free time to studying health and fitness to improve my own well-being. It wasn't until I joined the Marine Corps that I realized how many people face the same fitness challenges.

While I was in the Marine Corps, I got a job helping out Marines who didn't quite meet our fitness standards. It was like a dream gig for me.
In that year, I helped over a hundred Marines get back on track with their fitness, even some who were way over the limit. Meanwhile, I kept learning about fitness so I could share the info and help them out.

hence the theme of this whole website


Mission statement:

First and foremost this is going to be a dual purpose website/project, I want this to serve as both a résumé and also a fitness tool for anyone who may need it.
