Mission statement

My goal with this website is to spread useful and accurate Information regarding general health and physical fitness, to better help reinforce good decisions in regards to a persons overall wellbeing.


Within this website I will provide to you:

  • A Body Mass Index Calculator that clearly shows your personalized BMI

  • A Nutrition Information segment so that you know what to do with your now known BMI

  • an FAQ segment in my "About" segment to further assist you with applying these tools

  • A weekly Newsletter on topics related to fitness and/or cookbook recipes you could find useful (currently unavailable)

  • bullet points outlining tools afforded to the user
