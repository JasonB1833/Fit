Mission statement

With the digital age growing endlessly, misinformation spreads without faulter affecting millions in all aspects of life including their day-to-day health. In this vast sea of misleading information even a droplet of accurate science backed information could help influence countless lives. My goal with this website is to spread useful and accurate Information regarding general health, physical fitness, and nutrition to better help reinforce good decisions in regards to a persons overall wellbeing.


Within this website I will provide to you:

  • A Body Mass Index Calculator that clearly shows your personalized BMI along with a short description of what that is.

  • A Nutrition Information section so that you know how to implement this knowledge

  • an FAQ segment in my "About" segment to further assist you with applying these tools

  • A weekly Newsletter on topics related to fitness and/or cookbook recipes you could find useful (currently unavailable)
